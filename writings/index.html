You can mix and match grid-style links and list-style links on this page. Title images are not required for grid-style links, but they make things look cooler.

To add a new section of links, you can create an <h2> tag with your section title, then a div with either class="article-grid" or class="article-list". For each new article entry in this section, create an <article> tag within the div (see examples below).

Note: Having paragraphs of explanation text before each section (like what is done for this intro section) is totally optional. You can just alternate between <h2> and <div class="grid/article-list"> to make sections, or even just have one big <div class="article-grid/article-list"> for all articles on the page.
